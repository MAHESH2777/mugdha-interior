Consultation



Architectural : - We provide the suitable design services for buildings and the space within the site for principal purpose of human occupancy or use.

Aesthetic: - While we provide the design, we look for the Aesthetics which makes the building efficient use and ergonomically.

Vaastu : - We follow Vaastu principles in enhancing the natural lighting, air ventilation and safety at the place.



Designing




Site Visit: - Our work starts with Site Visit, during this phase; we even collect all the required measurements where we need to design for in the form of a checklist.

Space Planning: - Efficient space planning would be done in association with the client keeping in the mind their passion. We offer our custom design solutions to satisfy our client requirements innovatively in each stage from conception to completion.

Gathering of Requirements & Plans (Floor & Electrical): - We would be having detailed interview process with the client to gather the requirements of our clientele. We will collect the Floor and Electrical plan as part of first step.

Design Preparation: - Based on the inputs from meetings and interviews, our design professionals study the floor/electrical plans of the site and conceptualize to themes and schemes that can transform the site as per the needs of the clients.

Design Presentation: - The design concepts are then tranformed through rendered graphics and the same being presented to our clients for their visualization.

Design Refinements: - From the previous phase, we might get into refinements and the same would be carried until we reach to common consensus on the designs.

Design Finalization: - In this phase the final 3D designs would get prepared based on the themes agreed / approved by client. These 3D designs play vital role in sub-sequent phases.



Building



Blue Print & Material Selection: - we will create working drawings with exact measurements and parameters for guiding the work force in proper implementation for the future phases. The graphics of the conceptual design is also vital for material selection. Upon finalised design, suitable materials would select to bring in the same look and feel.

Milestone based deliverables: - Based on the material requirements in terms of quality, quantity, texture, colour, technical specifications, suppliers, price we arrive at delivery lead times. This exercise would be helpful while finalising the project timelines.




Customer Engagement



Finalization of Cost & Budget: - We will prepare the cost sheets based on the information collected in the previous stages. We will ensure these cost sheets are in line with the budgetary constraints given by the clients. If there are cost overruns then a special exercise is undertaken in re-negotiating deals, using cost effective substitutes etc., to fit the requirements within budget without compromising on quality and workmanship. Very rarely, the material requirements are modified to fit the budgetary constraints.

Proposal Preparation: - In the proposal preparation phase, with all the relevant inputs we prepare a proposal including implementation methodology, deliverable schedules, effort estimates, and cost requirements etc.,

Proposal Submission & Approval: - Proposal would be submitted and the same would be get wetted by client for any last-minute modifications. Once this process is concluded the cost negotiations would kick-start in amicable way which would lead to final approval of the proposal.



Project Preparation


Kick-start of the Project: - Upon approval of the proposal and receipt of the advance from client, the project would get kickstart.

Material Requirement Schedules: - Material requirement schedules being prepared to enable us to place orders and assure availability of material to contractors. To ensure that right quality and quantity of material is purchased, we do have a material inspection programme to ensure only quality material is procured and used in the project.

Engagement of Contractors: - We have very good relations with quality contractors that specialize in implementation of designs. Working with such contractors over a period of time has resulted in improved levels of understanding in working styles and quality specifications. In case client would like to bring in their own contractors with in the agreed budgets we provide comprehensive guidance and supervision.




Project Execution



Project Implementation: - We have a dedicated well-organized professional expert in monitor and guide the contractor’s team in the entire implementation process at each and every stage to achieve workmanship on the tasks we perform for our clientele. In this process, if we found any anomalies; we would immediately take corrective actions in time to avoid accumulated delays in meeting milestone deliveries, cost overruns and any wastages.

Effective Communication: - We know the value of Keep the Customer Informed (KCI). At each and every stage we establish effective communication through various channels based on the preferences of our clientele.

Milestone Deliveries and Approvals: - The ability to do a good proposal/plan alone will not satisfy our clientele, the execution of the project and timely delivery of scheduled milestones with an approval would create our brand in the market space.

Completion & Handing over: - This would be the last stage of our project, we would ensure completion of all the tasks against a set of comprehensive checklists at each stage of delivery. After satisfactory completion of checklist verification, the site would be cleaned and removed all the unwanted materials, which would lead to site handing over phase.
